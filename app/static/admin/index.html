Upload CSV

CSV must include columns artist and song (or title). Uploading replaces the song list. Lyrics for songs that already exist are kept; new songs get fetched from Genius.

Status

Session

Starts a new singalong by zeroing every song's vote count. The song list and cached lyrics are preserved. Connected phones lose their ✅ checkmarks.

Danger zone

Wiping deletes every song, lyric, vote, and the current-song selection. There is no undo.
